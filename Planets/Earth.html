Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. This is enabled by Earth being a water world, the only one in the Solar System sustaining liquid surface water. Almost all of Earth's water is contained in its global ocean, covering 70.8% of Earth's crust.

From the majestic mountains to the serene seas, Earth is a mosaic of breathtaking landscapes and diverse ecosystems. It nurtures and sustains an intricate web of life, where every organism, from the tiniest microbe to the mightiest whale, plays a vital role in the intricate dance of existence. Yet, amidst its beauty, Earth reminds us of our responsibility to cherish and protect this precious planet for generations to come.
