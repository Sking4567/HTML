Mercury

Mercury Image

Mercury is the first planet from the Sun and the smallest in the Solar System. In English, it is named after the Roman god Mercurius (Mercury), god of commerce and communication, and the messenger of the gods. Mercury is classified as a terrestrial planet, with roughly the same surface gravity as Mars.

It has a barren, cratered surface, experiences extreme temperature variations, and has a thin atmosphere mainly composed of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium. Mercury's surface is heavily scarred by impact craters and features such as cliffs and plains, hinting at its violent geological past.
